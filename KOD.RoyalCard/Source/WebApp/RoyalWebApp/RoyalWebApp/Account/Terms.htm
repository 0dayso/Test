GINC is a company engaged in the business of providing entertainment services and promoting performing arts through its, well known, entertainment and leisure destination called the Kingdom of Dreams (“said Premise” or “KOD”). KOD comprises of the Nautanki Mahal, Culture Gully, Showshaa Theatre and the IIFA Buzz Lounge, through which KOD furthers the objective of showcasing live entertainment, handicrafts, Indian culture, etc. The said Premise also has restaurants, bars, food & beverage stalls and venues for conducting events.

  1. The Programme

    1. The ‘Royal Card Membership’ programme (hereinafter referred to as the ‘programme’) is brought to you by The Kingdom of Dreams, Leisure Valley, Sector 29, Gurgaon, Haryana-122002, India (hereinafter referred to as “Kingdom of Dreams”)

    2. The programme allows eligible Royal Card holders to earn and accumulate points for their purchases, every time they add monetary value to the card, which can be used against food and beverage, retail purchases at Culture Gully, purchase of tickets for shows at the Nautanki Mahal or any other service offered within Kingdom of Dreams, as determined by the Kingdom of Dreams management from time to time. Points accumulated on such Royal Card Membership Cards can be redeemed to pay for a wide variety of products and services available at Kingdom of Dreams.

  2. Eligibility for the Programme

    1. The programme is open only to individuals who are Indian nationals of 18 years or above, upon making a purchase of the minimum entry charge applicable to gain entry to the Kingdom of Dreams location. (Defined as individuals who have been accepted as members by Kingdom of Dreams and who have accepted the programme membership’s terms and conditions).

    2. Mandatory fields mentioned in the membership form provided at the Box Office must be filled by the member to be eligible to gain membership to the programme.

    3. The minimum monetary denomination of charges applicable for entry to Kingdom of Dreams may vary from time to time, as determined and at the discretion of Kingdom of Dreams management. The Kingdom of Dreams management also reserves the right to extend complimentary memberships to select individuals on its own discretion.

    4. Companies, partnerships, unincorporated associations, groups or other such entities cannot enroll in the Royal Card programme. Kingdom of Dreams reserves the right to grant or refuse membership to the Royal Card Membership programme.

    5. This programme is not open to any of the Kingdom of Dreams employees, or their immediate relatives (spouse, children, parents, brothers and sisters). The programme is also not open to any franchisees of Kingdom of Dreams, their associates and all Kingdom of Dreams employees who are associated with this programme directly or indirectly.

  3. Enrollment and Membership

    1. Enrollment and participation in the programme is voluntary.

    2. Membership can be obtained into the programme by completing and submitting the enrollment form , offered at the Box Office counter located at the entrance of Kingdom of Dreams and fulfilling the eligibility criteria mentioned in section 2 of the terms and conditions of the Royal Card Membership Programme.

    3. There are 2 types of memberships offered under the programme – (i) Primary membership where the individual alone is being enrolled into the programme, and (ii) Add-On membership where in addition to the primary card, another add-on membership card is offered to the member, wherein the add-on card is linked to the primary membership card account and charge spend carried by the members holding either of the card is accumulated in the primary membership card account.

    4. All membership benefits, expiry of membership or points, card upgrade or downgrade criteria will be collectively applicable to the primary membership card as well as the linked add-on cards issued. On tier upgrade to the next membership level both cards will be upgraded together. Similarly, at the time of membership expiry, both cards will expire together.

    5. There is no annual fee for participation in the programme.

    6. Any change of information as provided in the Royal Card Membership Programme enrollment form must be notified to Kingdom of Dreams Programme in writing.

    7. The member may opt out of the programme by providing a written intimation to Kingdom of Dreams at the Royal Card Membership help desk located inside the Kingdom of Dreams premises.

    8. The membership will be valid for 2 years from the date of enrollment, which will be extended in case the Royal Card membership holder carries out a re-charge of monetary value at Kingdom of Dreams within 12 months of his membership expiry date. In case the member is unable to fulfill the membership extension criteria, the membership expiry will occur on the completion of 2 years from the date of enrollment in the programme.

    1. On membership expiry, all balance points available in the member’s account earned during the membership period will expire also, if not redeemed.

    2. Kingdom of Dreams reserves the right to refuse membership to any applicant without assigning any reason.

  1. Membership Card Levels and Benefits

    1. Kingdom of Dreams reserves the right to add, modify or delete tier level benefits and/or qualifications at its sole discretion, with or without notice.

    2. In order to be upgraded to a Gold or Platinum tier a member has to complete that tier’s minimum amount of spend on charging the membership card carried out during the member’s tier period.

    3. Eligible points are base points that are earned by Kingdom of Dreams Royal Card members against eligible transactions (for details please refer to Accrual of points below). All points rewarded as bonuses to members are ineligible towards tier accrual.

    4. Member’s tier period is a 12-month rolling period starting from the date of membership. Points accrued towards tier upgrades are lapsed on completion of each 12-month rolling period.

    5. The Kingdom of Dreams Royal Membership Programme currently has the following membership tiers: Purple, Blue, Gold, Platinum and Titanium.


Membership Card Levels

    1. Royal Purple Card

      1. The Purple Card will be the base tier level and all new members will be enrolled in this tier.

      2. The Royal Purple Card members will be awarded 5 points for every eligible INR 100 (Rupees one hundred only) spent.

      3. Renewal of Purple Card membership – Since this is the base level card membership, tier maintenance is not applicable to this level. However, to retain membership, the member will have to carry out at least one spend transaction in the preceding 12 months, prior to the date of membership expiry.

    2. Royal Blue Card

      1. The Blue Card is the next level above the base tier level and all members who achieve a cumulative spend of Rs.10,000 in a 12 month rolling period from the time of membership, at Kingdom of Dreams, are eligible to be upgraded to Royal Blue Card membership.

      2. The Royal Blue Card members will be awarded 7.5 points for every eligible INR 100 (one hundred only) spent.

      3. Members can retain their Blue Card tier level by carrying out a minimum cumulative charge spend of Rs. 10,000 in the succeeding 12 months rolling period of membership. If the tier retention criteria are not met, the member will be downgraded to the Blue or Purple Card members tier.

    3. Royal Gold Card

      1. All members who have achieved at least Rs. 25,000 in a 12 month rolling period from the time of membership, at Kingdom of Dreams, are eligible to be upgraded to Royal Gold Card membership.

      2. The Gold members will be awarded 10 points for every INR 100 (one hundred only) spent at Kingdom of Dreams.

      3. Members can retain their Gold Card tier level by carrying out a minimum cumulative charge spend of Rs. 25,000 in the succeeding 12 months rolling period of membership. If the tier retention criteria are not met, the member will be downgraded to the Blue or Purple Card members tier.

    4. Royal Platinum Card

      1. All guests who have achieved at least Rs. 50,000 in a 12 month rolling period from the time of membership, at Kingdom of Dreams, are eligible to be upgraded to Royal Platinum Card membership.

      2. The Gold members will be awarded 12.5 points for every INR 100 (one hundred only) spent at Kingdom of Dreams.

      3. Members can retain their Gold Card tier level by carrying out a minimum cumulative charge spend of Rs.50,000 in the succeeding 12 months rolling period of membership. If the tier retention criteria is not met, the member will be downgraded to the Gold, Blue or Purple Card members tier.

    5. Royal Titanium Card

      1. All members who have achieved at least Rs.1,00,000 in a 12 month rolling period from the time of membership, at Kingdom of Dreams, are eligible to be upgraded to Royal Titanium Card membership.

      1. The Titanium members will be awarded 15 points for every INR 100 (one hundred only) spent at Kingdom of Dreams.

      2. Members can retain their Titanium Card tier level by carrying out a minimum cumulative charge spend of Rs. 1,00,000 in the succeeding 12 months rolling period of membership; If the tier retention criteria is not met, the member will be downgraded to the Platinum, Gold, Blue or Purple Card members tier .

  1. Accrual of Points

    1. 5 base points for every Rs.100 will be earned by the member for every monetary charge to their Royal Card at Kingdom of Dreams. These points may vary and will be applicable for certain defined products/categories. These products/categories can change from points earned at the sole discretion of Kingdom of Dreams.

    2. While making purchases at the Kingdom of Dreams, members need to present their membership card to the cashier before billing. Accordingly, points earned on eligible spends will be credited to your membership account upon presentation of your ‘Royal Card Membership’ card.

    3. Points accumulated by a member on his/ her Royal Card cannot be combined or used in conjunction with any other offers at the time of redemption.

    1. The points do not constitute property of the member and are not transferable by operation of law or otherwise to any other person or entity except to an Add-On card linked to the primary membership card.

    2. Members cannot accrue points for any purchases incurred prior to his/her membership date.

    3. In the event the Royal Card Membership Card is voluntarily closed by the member, the points accumulated on his/her Royal Card can be redeemed within 3 months of closure, otherwise these will automatically lapse. In the event of cancellation of the Royal Card Membership Card for any other reason, all the points accumulated will stand forfeited. If the Royal Card Membership Card is blocked or suspended by Kingdom of Dreams for any reason whatsoever, then the points accumulated shall stand forfeited, but may be reinstated at the discretion of Kingdom of Dreams if the use of Royal Card Membership Card is reinstated.

    4. The allotment and redemption of points will be solely at the discretion of the Kingdom of Dreams management.

    5. Kingdom of Dreams’ computation of points shall be final, conclusive and binding and will not be liable to be disputed or questioned, except in manifest error.

    6. Points will not be awarded or earned in the following cases:

      1. Special offers / promotions / items excluded by the management.

      2. All or any gift vouchers issued.

      3. Credit notes transactions, since points are not deducted when return of good are made.

    1. Kingdom of Dreams reserves the right to withdraw any one or all the cards issued by them. On a card being withdrawn, Kingdom of Dreams will be responsible to the card holder only to the extent of the points in the credit of the cardholder’s account.

    2. Bonus points can be earned on special product categories. These categories can change at the sole discretion of Kingdom of Dreams.

    3. Base points can be earned on the purchase of certain defined products. These products may vary, based on the discretion of Kingdom of Dreams.

    4. Programme members cannot transfer any points to another person.

  1. Points Expiry

    1. Points accumulated in the first year of becoming a member that are not redeemed by the member against products and services offered at Kingdom of Dreams till the end of his second membership year on the date he/she became a member shall lapse and be forfeited. The same will apply if the membership of the individual will be extended to the 3rd, 4th year and so on.

    2. Points once lapsed/forfeited will not be re-credited to the member.

    6. Any gift vouchers issued to members are valid for redemption only at the Kingdom of Dreams.

    7. Member's signature is subject to verification at the time of purchase or redemption.

    8. No cash refund will be entertained for purchases made on your membership card.

    9. If the gift vouchers issued against the points are lost, Kingdom of Dreams will not be responsible and no duplicate gift vouchers will be issued in this regards.

    10. Points once redeemed against a purchase can in no event be re-credited.

    11. Points cannot be redeemed for cash.

    12. Redemption can be executed only after the member has accumulated a minimum balance of points. This minimum balance of points for redemption may be changed by Kingdom of Dreams without giving any notice.

    13. No accumulation or redemption of points will be permissible if, on the relevant date, the card has been withdrawn, cancelled, is liable to be cancelled or if there is any breach of any clause of the terms and conditions. The points will lapse on the date of closure/cancellation of card.

    14. On redemption, points so redeemed would be automatically deleted from the accumulated points in the member’s card.
